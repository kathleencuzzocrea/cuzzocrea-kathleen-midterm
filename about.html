About Kathleen's Consoles

Founded by Kathleen Cuzzocrea in 2025 for a wireframing assignment, Kathleen’s Consoles is a fictitious game store inspired by real life frustrations with unpleasant interactions at local game stores. There simply aren’t enough spaces in this world where a girl can buy a Nintendo Wii game in peace these days. Kathleen’s Consoles promises an uncomfortable-small-talk and misogyny-free shopping experience while also offering an endless library of games for every type of gamer to enjoy.

Our Library

We are proud to offer an extensive library of games from every genre imaginable for every console generation and beyond. A third-party Wii game you played once at your friends house in 2009? A semi-obscure Playstation RPG released in Japan for only one week in 1997? Plain old Minecraft? We’ve got it. And more! In addition to games we also have a collection of consoles and accessories for every gaming need. Thank you for supporting Kathleen's Consoles and doing your part in keeping physical game releases alive.
